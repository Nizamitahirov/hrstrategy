HR Department: 2025 Strategic Overview

Structure KPIs: What We've Achieved

Summary of HR Department's key accomplishments for the 2025 action plan.

Structure & Cost Optimization

  • Job analysis completed and structural changes implemented for Commercial, Finance, and Property departments.
  • A systematic framework for organizational design and change management has been defined.
  • Achieved ~20% reduction in mobile call expenses and optimized other administrative costs.
  • Restructured several key teams (Data, InfoSec, Investigation) for better efficiency.

Performance & Bonus System

  • The core concept and processes for the new Performance Management system are designed.
  • New KPI-based bonus systems successfully launched for Cashiers, Wolt, Real Estate, and other teams.
  • A comprehensive company-wide grading project was initiated and job role mapping completed with WTW.
  • Store Managers' performance evaluation tool created and deployed.

Digitization & Process Automation

  • Redesigned and automated key processes like Business Trip Requests and Vacation Management.
  • Developed and launched new web-based tools for Planogram QR, Headcount Planning, and Encashment.
  • LMS foundation established, including server setup and needs definition.
  • Significant progress in developing new applications for Uniform Distribution and Working Schedules.

Employee Development Programs

  • Enhanced the onboarding process with welcome kits and optimized meetings.
  • Launched new development programs for Store Managers, Area Managers, and SMAs.
  • Completed key leadership trainings, including a new handbook for Store Managers and DNA training.
  • Established an ongoing process for assessing learning needs across departments.

Organizational Culture & Engagement

  • Annual people survey completed to gather employee feedback.
  • A wide range of ongoing engagement events are being held to build strong teams and leadership community.
  • "My Bravo Story" EVP project initiated to strengthen employer brand.
  • Customer-centricity transformation program launched with posters and trainings.

Policy & Business Procedures

  • New Recruitment Policy and Job Description Management Procedure prepared and formalized.
  • Review of existing and development of new HR policies is actively in progress.
  • Established a Disciplinary Committee to ensure fair and collegial decision-making.
  • Travel Policy and Mobile Usage Policy updated and implemented.

Structure Activities: How We Affect the Business

Translating HR initiatives into tangible business value and strategic impact.

Driving Financial Performance & Productivity

  • Optimizing operational costs across administrative, travel, and mobile usage, directly contributing to bottom-line savings.
  • Increasing workforce productivity through systematic job analysis and structural redesign in key departments.
  • Ensuring competitive and sustainable compensation by implementing a new grading structure and salary benchmarks.
  • Boosting sales-driven roles' performance with new KPI-based bonus systems for cashiers, real estate, and more.

Enhancing Operational Excellence

  • Automating core HR and administrative processes to reduce manual work, minimize errors, and accelerate decision-making.
  • Standardizing business procedures (SOPs) to ensure consistency, improve compliance, and simplify operations across all locations.
  • Enabling faster new store roll-outs through efficient mass recruitment and streamlined onboarding, directly supporting business expansion.
  • Improving internal service quality by digitizing tools for vacation, uniform distribution, and other employee requests.

Strengthening Organizational Design & Planning

  • Developing agile organizational design frameworks that allow the company to adapt its structure quickly to market changes.
  • Implementing headcount planning systems to align staffing with sales forecasts, preventing overstaffing and maximizing ROI.
  • Defining clear career roadmaps and promotion policies to structure internal growth and increase transparency.
  • Building a robust succession plan for key roles to secure critical expertise and mitigate leadership gaps for long-term stability.

Building Future-Ready Talent & Leadership

  • Creating a sustainable leadership pipeline through targeted development programs (SM, AM) for long-term business continuity.
  • Reducing external hiring costs and increasing retention by cultivating internal talent through mentorship and training.
  • Attracting the next generation of professionals through structured internship programs, securing future capabilities.
  • Equipping the workforce with necessary skills through e-learning platforms (LMS) and needs-based training.

Fostering a High-Performance Culture

  • Boosting employee engagement and morale through recognition programs (Bravo League), leading to lower turnover and higher service quality.
  • Driving a customer-centric mindset across the organization with targeted training, directly impacting customer satisfaction and loyalty.
  • Strengthening the employer brand (EVP) to attract top-tier talent and become an employer of choice in a competitive market.
  • Fostering collaboration and innovation that drives business growth from within through regular engagement events and communication.

Mitigating Risk & Ensuring Compliance

  • Reducing legal exposure by aligning all internal processes with labor legislation and establishing a formal disciplinary committee.
  • Ensuring a safe working environment for all employees and customers through rigorous HSE monitoring, inspections, and training.
  • Implementing robust policies for asset management, data security, and contract governance to protect company resources.
  • Guaranteeing fair and consistent treatment of all employees through standardized HR procedures, minimizing workplace disputes.

Structure Roadmap

Our Proven Strengths and Key Strategic Challenges for the Future.

What We Do Well

Agile Recruitment & Expansion Support

We consistently meet aggressive hiring targets for new stores (e.g., Ismayilli, Shusha), demonstrating our capacity to directly support rapid business growth.

High-Impact Employee Engagement

A well-established calendar of diverse engagement activities and communication channels keeps morale high and fosters a strong leadership community.

Solid HR Operations & Execution

Core operational tasks, including reorganizations, salary adjustments, and compliance with regulations, are managed effectively and on time.

Proactive HSE & Safety Management

Our systematic approach to safety, including regular inspections, and incident tracking, has built a strong foundation for a safe workplace.

Foundation of Digital Transformation

We have successfully initiated and completed several key automation projects, proving our capability to digitize processes.

Challenges & Strategic Focus

Deepening Automation & System Integration

Our challenge is to move from standalone tools to a fully integrated HR ecosystem (e.g., full 1C automation, LMS) to unlock maximum efficiency.

Evolving Performance Management

The goal is to transition from a conceptual framework to a fully embedded system that links performance directly to development and rewards.

Finalizing & Embedding Policies

The strategic focus is on finalizing, communicating, and ensuring consistent application of all new policies across the organization.

Leveraging HR Analytics for Strategy

We collect valuable data. The next step is to build analytics capabilities to transform this data into predictive insights that inform strategic business decisions.

Building a Formal Career Architecture

A key priority is to design and implement clear, formal career paths and competency models for structured internal growth and retention.
